About Me

About me

My name is Piotr. I study Electronics and Telecommunications at Gdańsk University of Technology (WETI), currently in my 6th semester on the Computer Electronic Systems profile. What sets me apart is structural thinking - I break every problem down like a tree: category, subcategory, and the connections between them. I learn new things the same way, sorting information into clear categories instead of letting it pile up as chaos. Automation and chaining tools into systems aren't a separate skill - they're just what that mindset looks like in practice, the thread that ties everything else together.

What keeps me busy right now:

  • Engineering thesis - teaching a reinforcement-learning agent (PPO/DQN) to master an arcade game on its own
  • SimLE research club - lead software developer of a 3 m radio telescope: remote-control web app, star targeting and tracking, SDR data analysis
  • Personal projects - a job search aggregator, this portfolio and a few websites built for real clients
  • Math tutoring - from primary school up to advanced high-school level

After hours you'll find me into geography, blending music, working on my fitness and diet at the gym, experimenting with AI audio-separation tools, or just getting started with architectural visualization in ComfyUI.
